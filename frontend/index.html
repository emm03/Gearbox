What is Gearbox?

Gearbox is an AI-powered assistant built specifically for retail environments where employees are expected to explain increasingly technical products with limited training time.

Instead of memorizing specs or relying on guesswork, Gearbox helps employees translate complex product information into clear, confident explanations in real time.

Designed for the Sales Floor

Gearbox is optimized for fast, real conversations — not long manuals or dense documentation.

Consistency Without Scripts

Customers receive accurate, consistent information without employees sounding robotic or scripted.

Real Store Scenarios

Gearbox is designed around the situations that slow teams down, create uncertainty, or lead to missed sales opportunities.

New Hire, Busy Shift

When a customer asks about an unfamiliar product, Gearbox provides a clear explanation, key differences, and common use cases in seconds.

High-Stakes Comparisons

Customers choosing between similar products get side-by-side clarity without technical overload or pressure.

Edge-Case Questions

Gearbox fills in knowledge gaps when questions go beyond standard training materials.

Confidence Under Pressure

Employees stay calm and credible during rushes instead of deferring or guessing.

Core Features & Philosophy

Gearbox is intentionally focused. It is built to support human interaction, not replace it.

Dual Explanation Modes

Switch between internal, training-oriented language and customer-facing explanations depending on the conversation.

Product-Aware AI

Responses are grounded in real product specs, categories, and intended use — not generic AI answers.

Reduced Training Load

New employees ramp faster without memorizing catalogs or relying on constant supervision.

Expandable by Store

Retailers can tailor Gearbox to their product lines, terminology, and training standards over time.
